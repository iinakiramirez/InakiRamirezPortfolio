Túnel 2/9/2021

Túnel is a product of my innocence as an artist. An attempt to prove what I was capable sonically and musically, through the use of new experimental genres and tools. In this EP I explore and experiment with genres that, at the time, were new for me, such as Techno, IDM and Glitch.
